# One Minute of You — Public Launch Kit

## Collection statement

One Minute of You is a deterministic generative art collection shaped by sixty seconds of human movement. A gesture is recorded locally, measured through kinematics and geometry, serialized into a SHA-256 seed, and resolved into a singular work of art.

You are not drawing. You are introducing a disturbance into a mathematical universe. The artwork is how that universe settles.

## Social post — science

Every One Minute of You work is reproducible.

Motion becomes kinematic features. Features become a SHA-256 seed. The seed resolves into a House, an algorithm, and a permanent portrait.

The same seed always returns the same universe.

## Social post — privacy

Your minute stays with you.

Interaction data is recorded and analyzed locally in your browser. Nothing is uploaded unless you decide to mint the finished work.
